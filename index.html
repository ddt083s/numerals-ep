Numerals EP

Vol. 01 - Synergy EP

Start

“Start” launches the EP with chaotic energy and poetic arrogance. A debut statement that mixes rebellion and reflection, warning that this new artist is here to stay.

Zero

A venomous takedown of addiction, society, and self. Dark, heavy, and deeply personal — it’s the EP’s most brutal catharsis.

Numerals

Introspective and intense, “Numerals” tackles personal struggles with time, identity, and creative expression. A thoughtful and poetic core of the EP.

Finite

With rapid-fire wordplay and smart metaphors, “Finite” explores the limitations of life and legacy. Sharp and conscious with layered meanings.

Infinity

Hopeful, philosophical, and emotionally aware — “Infinity” offers elevation and empowerment, with a clever chorus flip to close the story arc.

Finite (Infinite Remix)

This remix overlays Eminem’s “Infinite” beat with the “Finite” message, blending homage and originality into a finale that echoes past and future.
